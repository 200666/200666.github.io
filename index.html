Asia

Asia was where I was born and, therefore, I have a deep connection to all things related to it's culture. This section includes photos from places all over Asia including: Hong Kong, Beijing, and other places I have visited with my family. Most of my photos come from Hong Kong, because it is where I spend most of my time. The art is not mine, but instead street art that I believe is beutiful and deserves reconition.

America

America is where I am from, but it is not my home. Although I have a American passport and spend most vacitons there, I will still always be connected with Hong Kong. American culture does influence all aspects of my life, so I still take pictures all over there. The following photos are from all over the east coast of America: New Jersy, New York, Delaware, Massatuchets, and more. This is where I feel most represents me and my family.
